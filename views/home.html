More About Us

We build products to help dogs relate to their families. One of the innovative projects we have started is a database of ways in which dogs communicate. We plan to use this data to develop a translation algorithm for humans to be able to communicate with their beloved four-legged friends.


Everyone here at ZiggyCorp loves dogs. We are a dog-friendly workplace and we encourage all all of our team members to bring their dogs to work with them. We have a large dog run on our campus and our break room is stocked full of healthy dog treats.

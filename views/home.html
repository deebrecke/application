About the Internship

We are searching for a software development intern who is an innovative future-thinking dog-lover. At ZiggyCorp, it's all about the dog.


More About Us

We build products to help dogs relate to their families. One of the innovative projects we have started is a database of ways in which dogs communicate. We plan to use this data to develop a translation algorithm for humans to be able to communicate with their beloved four-legged friends. We are also working toward developing VR and AR virtual trainer apps.


Everyone here at ZiggyCorp loves dogs. We are a dog-friendly workplace, and we encourage all of our team members to bring their dogs to work with them. We have a large dog run on our campus and our break room is stocked full of healthy dog treats. We have a mobile groomer that comes in once a week, at the company expense.

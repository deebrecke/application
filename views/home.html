About the Internship

We are searching for a software development intern who is an innovative future-thinking dog-lover. At ZiggyCorp, it's all about the dog.


More About Us

Our software department builds products to help dogs relate to their families. One of the innovative projects we have started is a database of ways in which dogs communicate. We plan to use this data to develop a translation algorithm for humans to be able to better communicate with their beloved four-legged friends. We are also working toward developing VR and AR virtual trainer apps.

In addition to our innovative software, we provide full-service care for all of our customers' dogs needs. These services are on site and include veterinary services, trainers (behavioral and agility), groomers, nutritionists, and boarding. We also have a great retail outlet with all the best treats, food, toys, and equipment. Don't see it in the store? Just ask and we will order it.

Everyone here at ZiggyCorp loves dogs. Our offices are all dog-friendly, and we encourage all of our team members to bring their dogs to work with them. As part of our benefits package, all team members receive vouchers to take part in any of our dog services. When you bring your dog to work and need a break, you can board him for an hour or even the whole day. You also have access to open the dog park (multiple dogs at a time), the agility course, and the dog run (one dog at a time) any time you or your fur-baby need to get some energy out.
